About Meld

We are a Washington, D.C. based tech worker-cooperative focused on social change and economic freedom through the lenses of lasting vision, local shared economies, empowerment, diversity and inclusion.



If you want to build a community or want to join one. We would love to have you. We believe foresight, compassion and dedication can cultivate the space for the future to best manifest.

We offer technology services, consulting, event space, classes and workshops to help you achieve your full potential. Our craft has been honed through use of Agile, Human Centered Design + Lean Startup and evolutionary teal based management practices.

We are always looking for ways to grow the community, whether you're seeking to launch and grow a small business or a start-up. Through our unique incubator program the Crucible Project (coming soon) where we help you from ideation to building a team and supporters as well as connect you to service providers and mentors and eventually funding.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to grow and develop skills to grow their dreams. We operate under a sliding pay-scale model and for those in the community a pay-what-you-can program where we link you with opportunities to share or work in exchange for what you need.
