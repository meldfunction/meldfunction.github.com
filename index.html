About Meld

Being an entreprenuer or intraprenuer shouldn't be so hard...we believe that those driven by vision and purpose sometimes just need a place to go where they can feel welcome away from the crazyness of it all.

We are a Washington, D.C. based tech worker-cooperative focused on helping people try new things, develop new paradigms, experience social change and economic freedom through the lenses of purpose, cooperative economics, empowerment, diversity and inclusion.



If you want to build a community or want to join one. We would love to have you. We believe foresight, compassion and dedication can cultivate the space for the future to best manifest.

We offer technology services, consulting, event space, classes and workshops to help you achieve your fullest potential.

We use the services platform to support internal growth and to work on the things important to us and our members and find ways to grow the community. Through our unique incubator program the Crucible Project (2017) we help you build a community of mentors, supporters and experts to help you on your journey.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to grow and develop skills to grow their dreams. We operate under a sliding pay-scale model and for those in the community a pay-what-you-can program where we link you with opportunities to share or work in exchange for what you need.

The Crucible

We realized that the life of an entrepreneur can be difficult and stressful. Often times issues issues that founders run into at the beginning can multiply as they go along. The Crucible is a metaphor for the transformative life experiences that make us YOUnique. At Meld we strive to combine growth experiences with high aspirations for truly genuine outcomes. We currently offer three cohorts:

  • The Explorer
  • Dates: August-September 2016
  • Keywords: Discover, Interact, Distill
  • Cost: Time-exchange or $500
  • The Seeker
  • Dates: September-December 2016
  • Keywords: Dive, Insight, Share
  • Cost: TBD
    The Maestro
  • Dates: January-December 2017
  • Keywords: Give, Interconnect, Radiate
  • Cost: Negotiable

The Crucible, focuses on something that isn't always emphasized in the entrepreneurial journey: YOU.

We provide you with the room to grow whether you come in as a team or as an individual. Dealing complex or unknown situations often times starts with recognizing your character strengths. In research done at UVA, entreprenuers must develop key traits to unlock their potential. (If you're interested in learning more, check out Dr. Sarasvathy's TEDx Talk).

Therefore we have developed a unique curriculum based on the cooperative principles, crafting an entrepreneurial journey backed by industry best practices. Our carefully curated curriculum has been built by a full range of local-prenuers from the Open Masters program to the Startup Community (see DMV Startups). We encourage the intersection of knowledge sharing from as many sources as possible from pop-culture to Zen masters.

Our goal is to get participants out of their comfort zone to explore topics such as: Creative Leadership (see Lead-in), self-inquiry and mindfulness (see Vipassana), Mentoring (see Eastern Foundry, Village Capital), design+tech experts (see Creative-Cycle), Design thinking (see DT:DC and OpenIDEO), Agile (see IC Agile - and Agile Leadership Network), Coaching (see Energy Coach), Human-centered-design and Social Change Labs.

And in order to be a well-rounded entrepreneur wellness is key. We also provide opportunities and access to great loca-nomical food and catering (see Zenful Bites) as well as travel experiences.

We believe by enabling resources, community and creative awareness YOUnique growth is inevitable.
