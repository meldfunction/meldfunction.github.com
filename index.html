Meld()


Meld is a word that means to merge, blend, or unite and is a "portmanteau", a word formed by joining two other words which in our case is melt and weld. Function borrows from computer programming the purpose of our work. We believe by melting the differences between and welding the remainder within we can create change that is beautiful and harmonic as well as deep and awakening. We help individuals and communities grow. © 2016

About Meld()

We are a Washinton, D.C. based tech worker-cooperative focused on social change through the lenses of local economies, shared visioning, empowerment, diversity and inclusion.



If you want to build a community around the causes that matter most to you we organize local experts to help you achieve the next step.

If you are in need of help with your business, we provide technology services, consulting, classes and workshops.

If you are looking to start a small business or a startup we are developing a unique program called the Crucible Project (coming soon) where we help you go from A-Z and build a team, a network of supporters, service providers and mentors.

We believe that everyone should be able to benefit and not just the few, so we implemented a sliding payscale and a community pay-what-you-can model or share resources/skills in return.
